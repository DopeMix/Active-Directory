**MMC Customization**

1. Open a Blank MMC
- Press **_Win + R_** -> type **_mmc_** -> press Enter.
  This opens a blank Microsoft Management Console.
<img width="704" height="480" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/53eb4caf-87ba-4440-bfd6-808f6e8af45e" />

2. Add Snap-ins
- Click **_File_** -> **_Add/Remove Snap-in_**
- Add these snap-ins one by one
<img width="843" height="302" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a06a62c8-5469-4a2c-af44-45c95bec58aa" />

3. Save your MMC
- Click **_File_** -> **_Save As..._**
- Name it '**Admin Dashboard.msc**'
