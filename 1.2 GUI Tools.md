**ADUC (dsa.msc)**
<img width="892" height="627" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/29c04608-94ed-4861-9748-7c22cee5d308" />

- Purpose : Manage AD objects and structure
- Use it for:
  - Creating OUs, Users, Groups, Computers
  - Moving objects between OUs
  - Setting object attributes (department, email, login info)
  - Delegating OU adminitration
    
**_Who is where?_** - Easy GUI, foundational tool

Note : Delagation is the practice of selectively assigning administrative 
permissions to non-administrative users or groups, allowing them to perform 
specific, routine management task (e.g., password resets, unlocking accounts,
or managing group memberships) within designated organizational units (OUs).

**AD Sites and Services (dssite.msc)**
<img width="894" height="626" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/450f53ad-9d21-4082-83bd-a8f0949ed3a1" />

- Purpose: Manage physical structure of the network
- Use it for:
  - Sites -> physical locations / offices
  - Subnets -> map IP ranges to sites
  - Domain Controllers -> assign to sites
  - Replication schedule -> control DC-to-DC sync
  - Optimize authentication traffic
    
**_Where do DCs live and how do they talk?** - network-focused

**Active Directory Domains and Trusts (domain.msc)**
<img width="1026" height="708" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1f2f339a-a6b0-4e71-bff5-7526773e83f1" />

- Purpose: Manage logical relationships between domains and forests
- Use it for:
  - Create / Remove domains
  - Manage trusts (one-way / two-way)
  - Raise or lower functional levels (domain / forest)
  - Rename domains (rare, advanced)
    
**_Separate buildings and relationships in your company_** - high-level AD architecture

- **ADUC** -> bottom part: OUs, Users, Groups, Computers (**_OBJECT MANAGEMENT_**)
- **GPMC** -> links to OUs/domains/sites -> applies policies (**_POLICY MANAGEMENT_**)
- **ADAC** -> modern replacement for ADUC, bulk & PowerShell (**_OBJECT MANAGEMENT_**)
- **AD Sites & Services** -> Site, Subnet, DC placement, replication (**_PHYSICAL NETWORK & REPLICATION_**)
- **Domains & Trust** -> Forest / domain relationships (**_DOMAIN / FOREST RELATIONSHIPS & FUNCTIONAL LEVELS_**)
